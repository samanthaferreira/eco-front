About

The average person makes 4.3 pounds per day which is equal to 1569.5 pounds per person per year. If everyone tried to minimize their waste even by something as little as cutting out plastic bags and switching to reusable bags we can make a significant change in our enviroment. In this page you have the power to search for stores with minimal or non packaging. So, Will you be a world changer?
